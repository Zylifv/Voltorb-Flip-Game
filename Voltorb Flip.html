The goal of the game is to find all of the 1, 2 and 3 tiles without flipping over any Voltorb tiles (bombs). If you succeed in doing this you move on to the next level and keep the points you have earned, but if you uncover just one bomb you lose all of your current points, as well as half your total points. You also risk going down one or more levels. Good luck!
